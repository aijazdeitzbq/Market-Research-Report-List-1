<p><p>The Antimony Pentoxide Flame Retardant market is primarily categorized into two types: colloidal flame retardants and dry powder flame retardants. </p><p>Colloidal flame retardants are suspended particles that provide effective fire resistance while maintaining a stable consistency in various formulations. They are often used in coatings and polymers. </p><p>Dry powder flame retardants, on the other hand, are powdered forms that can be easily integrated into solid materials, enhancing their fire resistance without affecting the material's structural integrity. Both types are essential in improving fire safety across multiple applications.</p></p>
