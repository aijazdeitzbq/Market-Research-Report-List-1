<p><strong>The Aqueous Polyurethane Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion</li><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aqueous polyurethane resin market primarily consists of two types: aqueous polyurethane dispersion (PUD) and aqueous polyurethane emulsion (PUEM). Aqueous polyurethane dispersion involves the suspension of polymer particles in water, offering superior performance in coatings and adhesives due to its high stability and low environmental impact. Aqueous polyurethane emulsion, on the other hand, consists of water-based systems that provide excellent film-forming properties and adhesion, commonly used in paints, textiles, and various industrial applications. Both types are valued for their eco-friendliness and versatility.</p></p>
