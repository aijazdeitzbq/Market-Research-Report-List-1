<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Braking Systems market comprises various types designed to enhance vehicle safety and performance. Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during hard braking. Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD) optimizes brake force between front and rear wheels. Acceleration Slip Regulation (ASR) controls wheel spin during acceleration. Electronic Stability Program (ESP) enhances vehicle stability by detecting and reducing skidding. Electric Parking Brake (EPB) replaces traditional handbrakes with electronic control. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies and innovations in braking systems.</p></p>
