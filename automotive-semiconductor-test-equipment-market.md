<p><p>The automotive semiconductor test equipment market encompasses two primary types: wafer testing and package testing. Wafer testing involves assessing semiconductor chips at the wafer level, ensuring functionality and performance before they are cut into individual chips. This process is crucial for identifying defects early, thus enhancing yield and reliability. Package testing, on the other hand, evaluates the completed semiconductor devices within their protective packaging, ensuring they meet automotive standards for quality, durability, and performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
