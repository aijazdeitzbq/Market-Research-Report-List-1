<p><strong>The Automotive Vacuum Brake Booste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Diaphragm</li><li>Double Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive vacuum brake booster market is primarily segmented into single diaphragm and double diaphragm types. Single diaphragm boosters utilize one flexible diaphragm to amplify brake force, offering a compact design and adequate performance for most light vehicles. In contrast, double diaphragm boosters feature two diaphragms, providing greater efficiency and enhanced braking power, making them suitable for heavier vehicles or those requiring more robust braking systems. Both types are critical for improving vehicle safety and braking responsiveness.</p></p>
