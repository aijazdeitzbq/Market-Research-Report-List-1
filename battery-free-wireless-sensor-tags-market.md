<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Battery Free Wireless Sensor Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Tags</li><li>Passive Tags</li><li>Semi-active Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Battery-free wireless sensor tags are categorized into three main types: active tags, passive tags, and semi-active tags. Active tags generate their own power, often through energy harvesting methods, enabling long-range communication and more advanced functionality. Passive tags rely on external sources, such as RFID readers, for power, making them lightweight and cost-effective but limited in range. Semi-active tags combine elements of both, using a small battery for communication while leveraging energy harvesting for low-power operations, offering a balance of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
