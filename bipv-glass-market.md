<p><p>The Building-Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) glass market encompasses various technologies, including Dye-Sensitized Solar Cells (DSSC), Organic Photovoltaics (OPV), Amorphous Silicon, and Crystalline Silicon. DSSC offers flexible designs with lower efficiency, while OPV focuses on lightweight and aesthetically pleasing solutions. Amorphous Silicon provides low-cost manufacturing but lower efficiency, whereas Crystalline Silicon remains the most efficient and widely used. The market is also segmented into double modules, which maximize energy generation, and single modules, preferred for specific architectural applications, catering to diverse energy needs.</p></p>
