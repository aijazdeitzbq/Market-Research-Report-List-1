<p><p>The carpet and rugs market features various types based on manufacturing techniques. Woven carpets are created on traditional looms, offering durability and intricate designs. Tufted carpets are made by inserting yarn into a backing, providing a softer texture and faster production. Knotted rugs, often handmade, showcase craftsmanship with unique patterns and longevity. Needle-punched rugs are produced by mechanically entangling fibers, resulting in cost-effective and versatile options for various applications. Each type caters to different aesthetic and functional needs.</p></p>
