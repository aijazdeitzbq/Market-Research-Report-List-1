<p><strong>The Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Drainage Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventricular Drainage System</li><li>Lumbar Drainage System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) drainage systems market is categorized into two primary types: ventricular drainage systems and lumbar drainage systems. Ventricular drainage systems involve the placement of catheters into the brain's ventricles to manage excess CSF, often used in conditions like hydrocephalus. In contrast, lumbar drainage systems involve catheter insertion into the lumbar region of the spine to facilitate CSF removal, typically employed in cases of infection or post-surgical procedures. Both systems aim to alleviate pressure and optimize neurological health.</p></p>
