Nucleic Acid Test kits, including PCR tests, detect the virus's genetic material, providing accurate results for active infections. In contrast, Antibody Test kits identify antibodies in the blood, indicating past exposure to the virus, which aids in understanding immunity levels. Both types are crucial for diagnosing COVID-19, guiding treatment, and informing public health strategies.</p></p>
