<p><p>The DC ammeter market is primarily divided into two types: analog ammeters and digital ammeters. Analog ammeters use a moving coil mechanism to provide a visual indication of current flow through a needle and calibrated scale, making them simple and reliable for basic applications. Digital ammeters, on the other hand, utilize electronic components to display current readings on a digital screen, offering higher accuracy, additional features, and better readability. Both types cater to different user preferences and application requirements in various industries.</p></p>
