<p><ul><li>Fiber Core</li><li>Steel Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator wire rope market is primarily categorized into two types: fiber core and steel core ropes. Fiber core ropes utilize a synthetic or natural fiber core, providing flexibility and reduced weight, making them suitable for applications with lower loads. In contrast, steel core ropes offer enhanced strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty elevators that require higher lifting capacities. Each type serves specific operational needs, balancing factors such as load requirements, safety standards, and environmental considerations in elevator systems.</p></p>
