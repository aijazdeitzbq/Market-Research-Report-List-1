<p><ul><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li><li>Hall Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Engine Speed Sensor market features two primary types: Magnetic Electric and Hall Effect sensors. Magnetic Electric types utilize a magnet and coil configuration to generate a voltage signal proportional to engine speed, effective in harsh environments. Hall Effect sensors, on the other hand, rely on semiconductor technology, detecting changes in magnetic fields to provide accurate speed readings. Both types play crucial roles in engine management systems, enhancing performance, efficiency, and emissions control in modern vehicles, making them vital components across automotive applications.</p></p>
