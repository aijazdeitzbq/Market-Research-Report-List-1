<p><strong>The ETC (Electronic Toll Collection) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ETC</li><li>AET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) involves automated systems for collecting tolls without requiring vehicles to stop. In this market, there are two main types: ETC and Automated Enforcement Technology (AET). </p><p>ETC systems allow drivers to pay tolls electronically via transponders or license plate recognition, streamlining traffic flow. AET, on the other hand, focuses on enforcing toll compliance and traffic regulations using cameras and sensors, capturing violations like toll evasion, enhancing revenue collection and road safety. Both systems improve efficiency and minimize congestion.</p></p>
