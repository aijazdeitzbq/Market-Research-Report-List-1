<p><strong>The Fireproofing Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fireproofing sealants market is primarily categorized into elastomeric and intumescent types. Elastomeric sealants are flexible materials that provide excellent adhesion and resistance to temperature variations, making them ideal for sealing joints and gaps. In contrast, intumescent sealants expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a char that insulates and protects underlying structures from fire. Both types are essential for enhancing fire safety in buildings, ensuring compliance with regulations, and safeguarding lives and property against potential fire hazards.</p></p>
