<p><p>The magnetic conveyor market features various types, notably spiral and belt configurations. Spiral type conveyors utilize an intricate helical design, allowing materials to be transported vertically and horizontally in a compact space, ideal for limited areas. Conversely, belt type conveyors employ a continuous belt with magnetic properties designed to hold ferrous materials securely while moving them along a linear path. Both types enhance efficiency in material handling processes across diverse industries, optimizing productivity and product flow.</p></p>
