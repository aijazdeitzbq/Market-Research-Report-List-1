<p><p>The Medium and Low Vacuum Measuring Instruments market encompasses various technologies used to measure vacuum levels. The Thermoelectric Vacuum Gauge relies on temperature changes in a thermoelectric element to determine pressure. The Pirani Vacuum Gauge utilizes a heated wire and its cooling effect due to gas molecules for pressure measurement. The Film Vacuum Gauge employs a thin film capable of detecting changes in pressure based on mechanical deformation. Other instruments include ionization gauges and capacitance manometers, providing diverse measurement options for specific applications.</p></p>
