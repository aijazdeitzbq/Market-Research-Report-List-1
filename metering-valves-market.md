<p><p>The metering valves market consists of various types, including straight-through valves, two-way valves, and three-way valves. Straight-through valves allow fluid to flow in a single direction, optimizing flow efficiency. Two-way valves provide control over fluid direction and flow rate, making them versatile for various applications. Three-way valves enable the mixing or diverting of fluids, enhancing system flexibility. Additionally, other valve types cater to specific needs, contributing to the diverse functionalities required in fluid and gas management systems.</p></p>
