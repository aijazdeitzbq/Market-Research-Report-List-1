<p><p>MVHR (Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery) and PIV (Passive Input Ventilation) are advanced ventilation systems designed to improve indoor air quality while minimizing energy loss. MVHR systems extract heat from stale indoor air to pre-warm incoming fresh air, making them energy-efficient solutions for buildings. PIV, on the other hand, utilizes natural air pressure differences to bring fresh air into a space, improving ventilation without mechanical assistance.</p><p>The MVHR and PIV market are witnessing robust growth driven by rising awareness of indoor air quality, energy efficiency mandates, and increasing construction activities focusing on sustainable building practices.
