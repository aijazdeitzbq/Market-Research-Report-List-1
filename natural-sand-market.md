<p><p>The natural sand market is segmented into three key types: river sand, mountain sand, and sea sand. River sand is highly sought after for construction due to its smooth texture and availability. Mountain sand, obtained from crushed rock, is often coarser and used in specific construction applications. Sea sand, harvested from beaches, is less commonly used due to its salt content, which can negatively impact concrete durability. Each type serves distinct applications based on their physical properties and sourcing locations.</p></p>
