<p><p>The Neuromuscular Transmission Monitor market is categorized into two main types: Single Twitch Stimulation and Double Burst Stimulation monitors. Single Twitch Stimulation monitors assess the neuromuscular function by delivering a single electrical pulse, evaluating muscle response for clinical anesthesia management. In contrast, Double Burst Stimulation monitors provide two rapid bursts of stimulation, allowing for a more detailed assessment of neuromuscular block and recovery. These tools are essential in ensuring patient safety during surgical procedures where neuromuscular functions are involved.</p></p>
