<p><strong>The Neuroprosthetic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Output Neural Prosthetics</li><li>Input Neural Prosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neuroprosthetic system market is divided into two main types: output neural prosthetics and input neural prosthetics. Output neural prosthetics are devices designed to restore or enhance motor functions, such as artificial limbs that interpret neural signals to facilitate movement. In contrast, input neural prosthetics focus on interfacing with the nervous system to restore sensory functions, such as cochlear implants for hearing or retinal implants for vision. Both types aim to improve patients' quality of life by bridging gaps in neural function.</p></p>
