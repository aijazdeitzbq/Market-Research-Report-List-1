<p><ul><li>Strip Cut</li><li>Cross Cut</li><li>Micro Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper products shredders market is categorized into three main types: strip cut, cross cut, and micro cut. Strip cut shredders slice paper into long, narrow strips, providing basic security but can be easily reassembled. Cross cut shredders offer enhanced security by cutting paper into small confetti-like pieces, making reconstruction difficult. Micro cut shredders provide the highest level of security, shredding paper into tiny particles, ideal for sensitive information. Each type caters to varying security needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
