<p><p>The PET sheet extrusion line market is categorized into two main types: multilayer and single-layer. Single-layer PET sheets are created using a straightforward process, yielding a uniform thickness and structure, ideal for applications requiring clarity and greater flexibility. In contrast, multilayer PET sheets consist of multiple layers, allowing for enhanced properties such as barrier functions, mechanical strength, and customization. This versatility makes multilayer sheets suitable for a broader range of applications in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
