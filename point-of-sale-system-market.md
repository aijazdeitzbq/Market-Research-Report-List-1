<p><p>Point of Sale (POS) systems are categorized primarily into three market types: Desktop POS, Handheld POS, and Mobile POS. Desktop POS solutions are traditional systems installed on fixed computers, ideal for retail environments requiring extensive transaction management. Handheld POS devices are portable terminals that enable transaction processing and inventory checks on the go, enhancing customer service flexibility. Mobile POS systems utilize smartphones or tablets, allowing businesses to sell anywhere, offering convenience and improved customer engagement in various settings.</p></p>
