<p><strong>The Road Cold Recycler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asphalt Emulsion Recycler</li><li>Foamed Asphalt Recycler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Road Cold Recycler market consists of two primary types: Asphalt Emulsion Recyclers and Foamed Asphalt Recyclers. Asphalt Emulsion Recyclers utilize emulsified asphalt to enhance the recycling process, allowing for better bonding with reclaimed materials, improving pavement quality. Foamed Asphalt Recyclers, on the other hand, create a temporary foam by injecting water into hot asphalt, making it workable at lower temperatures. Both types effectively support sustainable road construction by enabling the reuse of existing materials, reducing waste and costs.</p></p>
