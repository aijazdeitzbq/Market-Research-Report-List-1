<p><p>The telemedicine stethoscope market includes two primary types: wireless and wired stethoscopes. Wireless stethoscopes utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit heart and lung sounds to healthcare providers remotely, enabling real-time diagnostics without physical presence. In contrast, wired stethoscopes connect directly to devices via cables, offering reliable sound quality and immediate feedback, but with limited mobility. Both types play a crucial role in telehealth, enhancing patient monitoring and facilitating remote consultations in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
