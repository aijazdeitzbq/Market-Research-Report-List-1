<p><ul><li>Middle Through Hole</li><li>First Through Hole</li><li>Later Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Through Silicon Via (TSV) equipment market is categorized into three main types based on the processing stages of hole creation in silicon wafers. Middle Through Hole refers to vias created during the middle stages of semiconductor fabrication, allowing for efficient interconnection between layers. First Through Hole involves initial hole drilling to facilitate inter-layer communication, while Later Through Hole focuses on vias formed in the final stages, enhancing substrate connectivity. Each type plays a crucial role in 3D integration and advanced packaging technologies.</p></p>
