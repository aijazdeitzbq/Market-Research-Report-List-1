<p><p>Urethane casting services encompass two primary market types: flexible urethane materials and rigid urethane materials. Flexible urethane materials are known for their elasticity, enabling them to absorb shocks and withstand bending, making them ideal for applications like gaskets, seals, and cushioning components. Conversely, rigid urethane materials offer robust strength and durability, suitable for structural applications such as automotive parts and durable consumer goods. Both types cater to diverse industries, providing tailored solutions based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
