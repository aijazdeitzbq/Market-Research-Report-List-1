<p><strong>The Water Cooled Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct</li><li>Indirect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-cooled resistors market can be categorized into two main types: direct and indirect. </p><p>In the direct market, water is in direct contact with the resistor, allowing for efficient heat dissipation and improved performance. This is commonly used in high-power applications. </p><p>The indirect market involves a cooling arrangement where water circulates through a secondary system to remove heat from the resistor. This type offers versatility and can be applied in various settings where space or installation constraints exist.</p></p>
